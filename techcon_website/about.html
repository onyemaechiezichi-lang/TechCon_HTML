History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of software developers passionate about sharing knowledge on open-source tools. Over the years, it evolved into one of the largest global technology conferences, attracting innovators, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders. By 2018, TechCon expanded into areas such as AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and cloud computing, becoming a hub for cutting-edge discussions and product launches. Today, TechCon continues to inspire thousands worldwide by connecting technology with real-world impact.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to: Empower innovators by creating a platform for sharing ideas. Bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical applications. Foster diversity and inclusion in the tech ecosystem by giving a stage to underrepresented voices. Shape the future through collaboration, mentorship, and global networking. We believe that technology should not only advance industries but also improve everyday lives.
